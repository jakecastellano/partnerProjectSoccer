There are many reason to play soccer, here are only a few:

1.Playing soccer is very beneficial to one's health. You might say, "But isn't every sport beneficial to one's health?". Yes it is, but in other sports their are constant breaks and timeouts while in soccer it is (mostly) non-stop 45 minute halves. Soccer demands that you be very alert and fast moving all the time because one second you'll take a shot on goal and the next you'll sprint back to your side and defend from the opponents.

2.Soccer is very easy to get into playing. With other sports you might have to be tough like in football or fast like in track, but in soccer there is always a ppsition for you. Are you slow but still want to play? Fine, you can play defense. Are you not tough and stiil want to play? Fine, you can play striker and not have to try to take the ball away from anyone. Maybe your both slow and weak but still want to play? Okay, you can be midfield and be support for both strikers and defense.

3.Soccer is a sport where you have to communicate with your team constantly. Being a team sport will let you learn better communication and team-work skills which you will need in everyday life and in jobs.
